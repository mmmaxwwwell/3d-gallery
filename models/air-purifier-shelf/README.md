Air Purifier Wall Shelf
=======================

A wall-mounted round shelf sized for the Levoit Core 200S air purifier
([Amazon B08FJ678YK](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08FJ678YK)). The 230mm
plate gives the purifier ~22mm of margin all around so the rubber feet
sit fully supported.

The shelf is braced by two diagonal truss struts that anchor to a flat
wall with four screws (two per strut).

Parts
-----

| File | Description | Material |
|------|-------------|----------|
| `shelf.stl` | Complete shelf (plate + struts, one piece) | PLA or PETG, ≥30% infill recommended |

Hardware
--------

- 4x #6 pan-head wood screw, or M3.5 wall anchor (drywall installs)

Library
-------

`air-purifier-shelf-lib.scad` holds all geometry and parameters. The
plate diameter (`shelf_diameter`) is the main customizer knob — change
it to fit a different purifier, but verify the screw counterbores still
clear once you do.

See [CLAUDE.md](CLAUDE.md) for assembly conventions and editing rules.
